About myself

Hi there, my name is Diana Staver.
Since the age of 7 I always loved playing with hair. When I was 15 I did the hair for a school presentation for a couple of girls, long before I had any specialty courses taken. I started an actual career in 2009 after I took a hairstylist course, and begun working soon after. Now, years after, I still strive to improve myself, by taking various courses related to modern hair cutting, coloring, styling, braiding techniques, as well as makeup.
My goal is to have every single customer leave with a smile on their face, while feeling beautiful both inside and out, and I do that by carefully listening to all of the client`s needs and desires.
